Our Story

At Sankalpa, every recipe carries the essence of our heritage. Our journey started in a humble South Indian kitchen, driven by a passion for pure, Sattvic ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and the timeless joy of feeding loved ones.

From signature, hand-ground chutney pudis to grand vegetarian feasts, we believe that authentic soul-satisfying food should not be lost to time. Today, we proudly bring that pristine quality and unmistakable warmth straight to your daily meals and grandest celebrations.

"The secret ingredient is always love... and perfectly roasted spices."

— Our Family Philosophy
